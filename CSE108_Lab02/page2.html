Training a Husky is Tricky!

Huskies are a pretty difficult breed to train. While they're smart, they are incredibly stubborn. However, it's not impossible to train them!

There are many great methods to training these incredibly smart and stubborn dogs, all of which can be easily found online.

An extremely well known person in the Siberean Husky group in the US has plenty of videos of tricks to train Huskies but also makes videos on guides to making dog-friendly treats for all weather occasions.

She also provides many updates on how to maintain your dog's health as huskies also are known for having sensitive stomachs when it comes to diets.

For instance, this video is an extremely helpful video she has on how to teach huskies a very important basic, potty training!
